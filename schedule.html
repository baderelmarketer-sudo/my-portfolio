Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our consultation

Is the consultation really free?

Yes! The 30-minute strategy session is completely free with no obligation. It's my way of understanding your needs and showing you how I can help.

What should I prepare for the meeting?

Just bring your marketing questions and goals! If you have access to your website analytics or current marketing data, that's helpful but not required.

How long is the consultation?

The consultation is 30 minutes, which is enough time to discuss your goals, review your current situation, and provide initial recommendations.

Can I reschedule if needed?

Absolutely! You can reschedule or cancel anytime up to 24 hours before the meeting through the confirmation email.

What happens after the consultation?

After our call, I'll send you a summary email with my recommendations. If we're a good fit, we can discuss next steps for working together.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Yes! I work with startups, small businesses, and established companies. Every business has unique needs, and I tailor my approach accordingly.
